Seattle Seahawks

The Seattle Seahawks plays at Lumen Field, formerly known as CenturyLink Field in the heart of downtown Seattle. They currently have one Superbowl under their belt and still are chasing for more. They defeated the Denver Broncos, 43-8, in Super Bowl XLVIII to win their first title in 2014.
